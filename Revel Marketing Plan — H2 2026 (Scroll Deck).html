01 · The Premise

H2 is structurally the strongest half of the year.

This plan sets the direction for Revel Saunas from 1 July through 31 December 2026. It builds on the momentum of H1 — EOFY Early Access, the Marty Fox partnership, the QLD Maroons Recovery Partnership, the Ultimate Wellness Stack — and extends Revel's positioning as Australia's premium home wellness brand into the four highest-leverage moments of the year.

Peak winter demand drives July and August. Father's Day anchors early September. Spring opens the lifestyle narrative through October. And Black Friday × Cyber Monday becomes the single largest revenue event of the half — every month before leads into it, every month after recovers with high-margin gifting.
